Bugs and Critters!

This is a small compilation of several different insects and creatures of the like that I find fascinating.

  • Insecta
  • Other arthropods

To be qualified as an insect, an animal must have: a head, a thorax, an abdomen, they have six legs, they have a pair of antennae, and they have zero or two pairs of wings.


You may think a critter is an insect, when it does not qualify according to these characteristics. The spider is the most common example.

Insects!

Bombardier Beetle

They have the infamous ability to synthesize and release rapid bursts of stinky, burning-hot liquid from their rear ends.

Acanthaspis Petax

The nymphs of this species create a pile of ant carcasses on their backs for camouflage.

Rosy Maple Moth

Distinguishable by their bright pink and yellow colors.

Giant Water Scavenger Beetle

Aquatic and big, as the name suggests!

Madagascar Hissing Cockroach

These animals release a hissing sound by expelling air when startled.

Death's-Head Hawkmoth

The startling skull like pattern on the thorax, behind the head, gave rise in folklore to it being known as an omen of death.

Colorado Potato Beetle

While they are considered a plague, these insects have a very beautiful pattern.

Praying Mantis

A very popular and memorable insect. Fun fact: they are famous for their sexual cannibalism.

Other critters!

Jumping Spider

With many species, this family of spiders are insanely charismatic!

Pill Bug

Furthest from a bug, these critters are actually most closely related to shrimp.

Ant-Mimicking Spiders

This family of spiders can fool even humans if you do not look closely.

Millipede

A huge group of critters, they come in several different colors and patterns, called morphs.

Pink Toe Tarantula

One of the most common species of tarantula to be kept as pets.
